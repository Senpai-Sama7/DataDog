# Sampling Plan Template

## Objective
State the decision and the metric to be estimated.

## Population & Universe
Define who or what you are studying. Include geography and time.

## Design
Choose: SRS, Stratified, Cluster, or Systematic. Justify in 3-5 sentences.

## Frame & Selection
Describe the sampling frame, inclusion/exclusion rules, and selection steps.

## Size & Error
State planned sample size and any margin-of-error calculation or constraint.

## Field Procedures
How data will be collected, quality checks, handling of non-response.

## Risks & Bias
List risks to validity and mitigations.

## Additional Resources
Hyperlink study material
